About Us

Bandons Dog Groomers has been a long running dog grooming service, providing the best of K-9 care to many dogs within the Bandon area of West Cork since it was established in 2005. We are a family run business that pride ourselves on the delivering the best treatments and grooming services to your K-9 friends, located just south of the Bandon Relief Road. We provide a variety of services and treatments inlcuding those listed below but not exhaustive to this. We also provide a Kennels service for those of you taking holidays and require a dog minding service while you're away. For any enquiries or requests you have , please do not hesitate to contact our friendly staff, Grace, Sean or Roberta by email at bandon-k9care@gmail.com or by telephone on 085-7654321, who will be more than happy to help you.
